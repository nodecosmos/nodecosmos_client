1. NODES TREE

Get Everyone on the Same Page

Structure your product as a tree of interconnected nodes. Each node can represent a system or its components, ingredients in a recipe, or any type of constituent depending on the nature of a project.

2. FLOWS

Processes That Make Sense

  • Easily model processes—from workings of systems and components to business procedures, supply chain logistics, and more.
  • In contrast to spontaneous flowcharts, our Flows interface is rooted in system theory and allows creation of realistic process models by defining node-to-node interactions in a step-by-step format.

3. DOCUMENTATION

Keep Knowledge at Your Fingertips

Every Node, Flow, Step, and I/O element can be documented on the spot using our real-time collaborative document editor. Capture critical information directly within context, ensuring clarity, preserving team knowledge, and making collaboration effortless.

4. BRANCHING & CONTRIBUTION REQUESTS

Drive Innovation with Peer-Reviewed Proposals

Propose changes, get peer reviews, and merge updates with project branches that track differences between current and proposed states of projects. Contribution Requests empower teams to propose innovations, ideas, optimizations, or any meaningful changes to projects.

4.1 Track Changes in Flows

Contribution Requests also track changes in Flows. This is critical in innovation, where understanding and tweaking interactions can lead to advancements in system design and functionality.

5. MODULAR MANAGEMENT

See Both the Forest and the Trees

Manage complex projects efficiently by breaking them down into manageable modules. With dedicated page for each node, teams can focus on their individual modules while maintaining a holistic view by navigating to parent nodes.

6. CROSS-FUNCTIONAL

One Visual Language to Rule Them All

  • Our node-based interface establishes a standardized visual language, that works across diverse fields. This enables stakeholders to understand and contribute to different parts of systems, regardless of their primary discipline.

7. OPEN SOURCE

Open source has forever redefined the landscape of technology by promoting collaboration, driving innovation, and fostering a culture of transparency. Its influence extends well beyond software, sparking groundbreaking changes in fields like education, healthcare, and community development. At NodeCosmos, we embrace this vision of collective progress across all aspects of product development—making our platform completely free for open source projects, whether they involve software or not.
