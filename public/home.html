Model, Document, and Evolve Products Together

NodeCosmos is your collaborative platform for product development, research, and knowledge management. With peer-reviewed contribution requests, maximize every team member’s impact while reducing costs and accelerating innovation.

1. NODES TREE

Simplify System Structure and Navigation

Manage and visualize your entire innovation ecosystem using our intuitive node-based structure. Nodes can be used to organize any constituent—systems, components, software, or even departments, labs, and their projects.

2. WORKFLOWS

Visualize and Optimize Your Processes

  • Easily model processes—from workings of systems and components to business procedures, supply chain logistics, and more.
  • Gain a realistic view of system workings by laying out interactions between nodes in a step-by-step format
  • Provide comprehensive overview with document on every flow, process step, and input/output element.

2.1 Progressively understand complex systems

With Workflow on every node, progressively comprehend innovation workings—from high-level overviews to deeply nested intricacies—allowing you to identify inefficiencies, and optimize performance.

3. CONTRIBUTION REQUESTS

Drive Innovation with Peer-Reviewed Proposals

Contribution Requests empower teams to propose innovations, ideas, optimizations, or any meaningful changes to systems or processes. Each proposal includes clear visual indicators of differences between the current and proposed states. Stakeholders can easily discuss and review the proposed changes, ensuring the best version of new ideas is implemented.

3.1 Track Changes in Workflows

Contribution Requests also track changes in Workflows. This is critical in innovation, where understanding and tweaking interactions can lead to advancements in system design and functionality.

4. MODULAR MANAGEMENT

Focus on Modules While Seeing the Whole Picture

Manage complex projects efficiently by breaking them down into manageable modules. With dedicated page for each node, teams can focus on their individual modules while maintaining a holistic view by navigating to parent nodes.

5. CROSS-FUNCTIONAL TEAMWORK

Utilize Standardized Visual Language

  • Our node-based interface establishes a standardized visual language, that works across diverse fields. Combined with documentation on every element, NodeCosmos empowers stakeholders to understand and contribute to different parts of systems, regardless of their primary discipline.
